HOW TO PLAY

GOAL — Survive 5:00. At 5:00, the Core spawns. Kill it to win.

MOVE with WASD or arrows. AIM with the mouse. FIRE by holding LMB.

BLINK with R or RMB — short teleport in your aim direction with iframes and a shockwave on exit. 3s cooldown.

XP shards drop from kills and pull toward you when close. Fill the bar to level up — pick one of three upgrades.

COMBOS — chain kills within 2 seconds to multiply score. The badge on screen shows your current multiplier.

ENEMIES: Runner (fast, weak), Bruiser (slow, tough), Swarmer (tiny clusters), Shooter (keeps range), Splitter (halves on death). Mid-boss at 2:30, Core at 5:00.

TIPS — never stand still; use blink to pierce through packs; XP is more valuable than HP at low levels; range upgrades change the entire feel of the game.
