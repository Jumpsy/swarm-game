How to play

GOAL

Survive as long as you can against an escalating horde. Every kill drops XP. Every level-up gives you a power. The longer you live, the harder it gets. Die → coins → leaderboard rank → run again.

CONTROLS

  • W A S D · move (also arrow keys)
  • Space · dash (short burst + 0.3s invincibility, 2.8s cooldown)
  • ESC · pause / resume
  • You don't aim — every weapon auto-targets the nearest enemy in its range.

WEAPONS (stack up to 6)

You start with the Pulse Rifle. Every level-up may offer a NEW WEAPON card (red border). Pick it to add it to your loadout. Pick it later again to level it up (1 → 5).

  • 🔫 Pulse Rifle — auto-fire at nearest enemy. Bullets. Lv3+ adds a second shot per burst.
  • 🗡️ Orbit Blades — discs orbit you and slice anything they touch. More per level.
  • ⚡ Chain Lightning — periodic AoE zap that arcs between enemies.
  • 💣 Proximity Mines — drops behind you. Explodes on enemy contact or after a timer.
  • 🔥 Flame Cone — forward cone DoT, auto-aims toward nearest enemy.
  • 🤖 Companion Drone — orbiting companion that auto-shoots its own bullets.

PASSIVES (buffs)

Common (white border), Rare (blue), Epic (purple), Legendary (gold). Most stack — the more times you pick the same passive, the stronger it gets. A few are one-time legendary effects (e.g. Shockwave detonates on every future level-up).

  • Power, Boots, Pierce, Magnet, Crit, Vitality, Endurance, Scholar, Big Bullet, Multishot, Chain, Vampiric, Homing, Armor, Boom, Lucky, Shockwave

ENEMIES (9 types)

  • 🔴 Grunt — base chaser, swarms in numbers
  • 🟠 Swift — fragile but fast, hard to outrun
  • 🟣 Tank — slow but takes many hits
  • 🔷 Shooter — ranged projectiles, kite them
  • 💠 Exploder — rushes you and detonates AoE on contact
  • 🔵 Shielded — frontal shield blocks bullets — flank or chain-attack them
  • 🟢 Spitter — lobs splash projectiles — don't stand still
  • ⭐ Spawner — creates more grunts; kill on sight
  • 👹 BOSS — every 60s. Big health bar at top. Warning shows 5s before.

HUD

  • Time · Kills · Level · Wave · Combo (resets on damage; ×10 = popup)
  • Green bar = HP · Purple bar = XP to next level
  • Bottom bar shows your weapons (orange border) and passives
  • Bottom-right circle = dash cooldown. Glowing blue = ready.

DIFFICULTY SCALING

  • 0:00 — basic swarm
  • 0:12+ — Swift enemies appear
  • 0:30+ — Tanks
  • 0:45+ — Shooters
  • 1:00 — first BOSS (every 60s after)
  • 1:00+ — Exploders
  • 1:30+ — Shielded
  • 2:00+ — Spitters
  • 2:30+ — Spawners
  • Enemy HP scales 1.2% per second forever.

SETTINGS (⚙ button)

  • Master / SFX / Music — independent volume sliders
  • Screen shake — toggle. Off if you get motion sickness.
  • Show ad slot — toggle the 728×60 placeholder above the game

COINS & LEADERBOARD

  • Coins earned per run = kills × 10 + level × 50 + minutes × 20. Persist forever in localStorage.
  • Top 10 personal runs saved to local leaderboard (top-right 🏆 button). Sorted by time then kills. Your name highlighted.
  • "Change Name" in top nav to rename. Doesn't reset progress.

TIPS

  • · Keep moving. Standing still = death.
  • · Pick a build path: bullet spam (Pulse + Multishot + Crit), AoE (Lightning + Mines + Boom), or melee orbit (Blades + Armor + Vitality).
  • · Dash through enemy bullets — you're invincible for 0.3s.
  • · Take Magnet early — XP starts coming to you instead of you to it.
  • · Vampiric + high crit = lifesteal build, you become unkillable.
  • · Shielded enemies block frontal hits. Chain Lightning ignores their shield.
